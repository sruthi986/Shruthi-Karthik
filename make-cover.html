📋 Step-by-Step: Fix WhatsApp Preview

1 Wait for fonts to load, then click "Generate & Download" above.
This saves exactly cover-og.png at 1200 × 630 px.
2 Put cover-og.png in your website folder (same folder as index.html).
3 In index.html, the og:image URL is already updated to use cover-og.png.
4 Deploy: Push to GitHub Pages or drag-drop folder to Netlify.
5 Clear WhatsApp cache — go to:
  developers.facebook.com/tools/debug
  Paste your URL → click "Scrape Again" → try sharing now.
6 Or simply add ?v=2 to your URL when sharing:
  https://your-site.netlify.app/?v=2
This forces WhatsApp to fetch a fresh preview.
